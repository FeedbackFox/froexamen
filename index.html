Welcome to Retro Games Arcade!

"Retro Games Arcade is a webshop that's been created by Suzy Hanson after she felt like she wanted to expand her passion for retro gaming and bring that joy to more people than can currently access it. She's a 34-year-old, married to well-known youtuber Egorapper (Arin Berhow). Arin plays a lot of retro games on his youtube channel with his partner Danny, which only encouraged Suzy's hobby for retro gaming. She started gaming fairly early into her life, growing up with an SNES that she played a hell of a lot of Super Metroid on. She has managed to keep the SNES running ever since, and its remained one of her favorite consoles of all time. This is why she had decided to start Retro Games Arcade - Spreading the joy. She hopes to be able to reach as many people as possible with this website, so that everyone can enjoy their childhood games, or even start getting interested in older games in the first place.
